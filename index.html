How much do you do to help sea turtles and other marine animals?

** Green indicates bonus points as they are actions you actively take to protect turtles

  • Refuse plastic bags
  • Avoid using straws or bring your own straws
  • Bring your own utensils (instead of using one-time use plastic utensils)
  • Use reusable water bottles (instead of using PET bottles)
  • Avoid buying balloons
  • Bike / use public transport (instead of driving)
  • Eat sustainable seafood and know where they come from
  • Use matches instead of lighters
  • Reduce use of chemicals (Or find biodegradable alternatives)
  • Avoid fires on nesting beaches
  • Avoid taking planes
  • Avoid using beach furniture
  • Clean beaches (while walking on beach or while diving / snorkeling)
  • Conduct turtle surveys or submit sighting data
  • Spread awareness or educate people about turtle conservation
  • Report if you see things made of turtle shell or discourage people from eating eggs
